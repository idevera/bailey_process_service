What We Do

Legal Document
Delivery Services

We handle every aspect of legal document service so you can focus on your case.

  • 01

    Service of Process

    Personal delivery of summons, complaints, subpoenas, petitions for divorce, protection orders, personal injury, and writs. Served correctly, on time, every time.

  • 02

    Substituted Service

    When direct service is not possible, we serve authorized household members or other qualifying recipients per RCW Title 4.

  • 03

    Rush & Same-Day Service

    Urgent deadline? We offer expedited and same-day options to keep your case on schedule no matter the time pressure.

  • 04

    Service by Mail

    Certified and first-class mail delivery with full documentation and proof of service.

  • 05

    Declaration of Service

    Court-ready declarations returned promptly after every serve. Fully compliant with Washington State requirements and accepted by all Washington courts.
